So... How're You Doing?

Posted by Chris Roman on February 11, 2019

It has been ten days since I started this blog. I’ve put together some words in a specific order in hopes to reach my potential audience. Some might call it preaching. But, is that all that I am doing? Am I just preaching? I jokingly put the tag “Let me grab my soapbox real fast” on the page listing my posts. Time to put up or shut up as it were.

Line item number one: Applying for jobs.

Have I been? Have I applied? Have I put myself out there?

That’s a Texas size 10-4, good buddy - Wayne from Letterkenny

In fact, I had my first interview last Monday. I have a second interview scheduled. I may get the job, I may not. Time will tell, but that isn’t what’s important. What’s important is that I’ve been applying. Applying so much, that I got a bite! While I would love to get this job, I am beyond excited about the interview process. I am also terrified of the interview process. Why? Because I have no idea what all is going to happen. But, however this turns out, it’s a win. Even if I completely bomb this next interview. This is INVALUABLE experience. At least… that’s what I keep telling myself (I really want this job).

Line item number two: Staying Motivated

Have I been? Have I been getting stuff done? Have I found a way to motivate myself?

Yes and no. Let me explain.

Yes, I have been getting things done. Yes, I have been working on things and trying to learn new stuff. Yes, I’ve been keeping up with this blog and applying for jobs (see Line item number one).

Have I been efficient and maximized my time? No.

You’re probably asking yourself what I mean by being efficient. How do you efficiently keep yourself on track to learn a new skill? You don’t know what you don’t know, so how do you do it efficiently? You don’t, but you also don’t randomly browse the web for the best Old Fashioned Recipe, or youtube non-coding videos, or suddenly realizing you’ve been searching Amazon for “just the right gaming mouse” for the past hour when you were supposed to be reading about recursion or hash tables or how to handle a 504 error or any of the other stuff I need to keep sharp in my mind. So, what’s the next step? What can I do to fix this?

Schedule everything.

Sounds terrible right? It probably will be. For a few weeks, it’s gonna suck. But, you know what it will do? It is going to start as an Action. That Action will eventually turn into a Habit. That Habit will eventually become part of my Character. And, finally, my Character will become my Destiny.

This new career path is my destiny. It is what is going to get me and my family where we want to be. I’ll be working on that schedule this week, and I will have a link to it in my next post.

So, until my next post, here is to taking Action.
