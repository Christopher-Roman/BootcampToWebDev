Finding my place in the Tech World

Well, I finally did it.

I started a blog. For the most part, this blog will be focused around me trying to break into the tech scene post Web Dev Bootcamp. But, it won't be limited to that. I created this place for me to share my own insights on things that I am going through, working on, and in some cases struggling with.

I am hopeful that the stuff I have to say will be benificial for those who happen to find this place. Hopefully, the topics I touch on are topics I'm not alone in working through. With that said, please feel free to message me about things you're working on or working through. I may not always have an answer, but I'd be happy to offer any insights.

But, who knows if anyone will even read this. Who knows if what I have to say will help anyone. At the very least, I'm hoping to be entertaining, with some insightful tid bits and what nots.
